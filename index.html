Welcome to Futbolistic Statistic

Instructions

Futbolistic Statistic is a data visualization platform with simple and straightforward navigation

1) The first dropdown selects a season, and displays stats of multiple leagues for that year.

2) The second dropdown select a league for more in depth statistics.

3) The third dropdown goes in depth on each individual club in that league.

To reset all the dropdown menus, simply refresh the page.

Enjoy!
